Eros Area icon standards --

50% transparency on background.  
Font: PixelSix14 (Available here: http://www.dafont.com/pixelsix.font) 8 pt.
Two empty pixels above text
At least one empty pixel on left and right sides of text
For departmental areas: Label the top with the department name.
Abbreviations are preferrable to cutting a word in half.
If you can't find a suitable abbreviation for a long word, try to think of a synonym.
